Chapter 06 — Inference vs Training

What You'll Learn​

  • What training is — how models learn from data and how weights are updated
  • What inference is — how models generate predictions from fixed weights
  • How training and inference differ in cost, speed, and purpose
  • When to fine-tune a model vs when to stick with prompt engineering

Prerequisites​

  • Chapter 01 complete — you understand what an LLM is and how it generates text

How This Connects​

Every time you call an API, you're running inference. But when a model keeps failing your use case, you'll hear "just fine-tune it" — understanding the difference helps you know when that advice is right and when it's overkill.
