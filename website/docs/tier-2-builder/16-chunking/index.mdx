## How This Connects

Chunking is the step that comes before embedding in every RAG pipeline. A 200-page PDF must be split into retrievable pieces before it can be embedded and stored. The quality of your chunking strategy directly determines how well your RAG system performs — even with perfect embeddings and a great LLM, wrong chunk sizes will produce poor results. Chunking feeds directly into vector databases (Chapter 15), and is a prerequisite for understanding hybrid search (Chapter 17).
