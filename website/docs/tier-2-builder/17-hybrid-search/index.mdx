## How This Connects

Hybrid search sits at the heart of production RAG systems. Pure vector search fails for queries with specific technical terms, product codes, and proper nouns — things that semantic similarity doesn't capture well. BM25 fills that gap. Together, via Reciprocal Rank Fusion, they produce retrieval quality that consistently beats either approach alone. Most enterprise RAG systems (Elasticsearch, Weaviate, Pinecone) offer hybrid search as a first-class feature for exactly this reason. After this chapter, you can add re-ranking on top of hybrid results for even higher precision.
