## Why This Matters

An unguarded LLM endpoint will eventually be abused — by prompt injection, by off-topic misuse, or by users extracting information they shouldn't have. Guardrails are the engineering layer that turns an experimental prototype into a system you can ship with confidence.

Every commercial AI product has guardrails. Understanding how to build them — and their limitations — is essential for any AI engineer working on customer-facing applications.
