Text-only models are powerful, but the real world is full of images: invoices, screenshots, charts, diagrams, photos. Multimodal models bridge that gap — they accept images as input and reason about them just like text.

This chapter unlocks a whole class of applications: document processing, visual QA, UI testing, and more.
