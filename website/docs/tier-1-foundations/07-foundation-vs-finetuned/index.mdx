# Chapter 07 — Foundation vs Fine-tuned Models

## What You'll Learn

- What foundation/base models are and how they differ from instruction-tuned models
- What fine-tuned models are and when they are worth the extra effort
- How instruction-tuning and RLHF turn a raw base model into a helpful assistant
- The major model families (Claude, GPT, Llama, Gemini) and where they sit on the spectrum
- A decision framework for choosing between a general API model and a fine-tuned specialist

## Prerequisites

- Chapter 01 complete — you understand what an LLM is and how it predicts tokens
- Chapter 06 complete — you understand inference vs training costs

## How This Connects

Every model you call through an API is the product of multiple training stages. Understanding those stages tells you why a model behaves the way it does — and whether you need to go further and fine-tune it yourself.
